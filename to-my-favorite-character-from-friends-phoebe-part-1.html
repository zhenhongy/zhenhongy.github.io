Though not the major character, Phoebe still impressed me most because of her personality

Phoebe was not the major focus for most of seasons. She was kind of like Joey who hadn't known others until they lived in the same building. However, Phoebe moved away from that place and it made her even less close to others. Except Season 7 and Season 8, Phoebe was in the least number of scenes and had the least number of lines, comparing to other five characters. At the end of Season 6, Rachel burned down Phoebe's apartment and then Phoebe lived with Monica for half of season 7. It could partially explain she showed up a little more in Season 7, though she still spoke less than others.

However, she still gave me some most entertaining and impressive moments in the show. Like...


Girls, Girls, Girls


P: Hey, y’know what might cheer you up? R: What?

Among ten seasons, Phoebe was closer with girls than boys and Ross. Monica, the bond of social network, was in the same scene with Phoebe most (707 scenes). The second closest friend was Rachel who was in 670 scenes with her. Joey and Chandler were equally close to her. But...Ross. They did lived together in Season 7 for some episode and taught her how to ride a bike (probably the only scene, I could remember, that only about her and Ross in there).

Besides those five friends, 10 characters were in more than 10 different scenes with her among ten seasons. It includes:

  • Family members, Alice (10 scenes) and Frank (19 scenes)
  • Husband/ex, Mike (31 scenes), Gary (11 scenes), and David (12 scenes)
  • Friends' ex, Carol (11 scenes), Emily (14 scenes), Tag (10 scenes), and Janice (14 scenes)
  • The best supporting role, Gunther (28 scenes).

Mike/Husband and Frank/Brother (and Gunther/alwaysBeThere in some way) were Phoebe's closest person besides the friends.
